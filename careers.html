CAREER & JOB OPPORTUNITIES
WITH RHINO DEMOLITION

Rhino is a growing organisation constantly looking for people with enthusiasm and talent. We want to attract people who share our core values:

Mutual respect: relating with work colleagues, customers and suppliers in ways that openly show respect for the contributions and positions of others.

Teamwork: working collaboratively with others, sharing ideas and resources to get things done safely and professionally.

Trustworthy: This is walking the talk, doing what you said, keeping your promise, being reliable and dependable in working with others.

Flexibility: responsiveness in attitude and behaviour to changing circumstances.

If you believe you have what it takes to work with a group of people who share similar values and are committed to getting things done to fulfil our client expectations, we would very much like to meet with you.

Rhino is a company that rewards effort. We provide our employees with a uniform, technical and safety training, ongoing professional development and for those that excel, opportunities to advance within the company.

We are currently seeking potential candidates to work as:
  • Estimators
  • Supervisors
  • Labourers
(with green card and preferably with a driver's licence)
